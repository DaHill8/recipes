Ingredients:
 - Virgin olive oil
 - tomatoes
 - dry basil
 - salt
 - black olives
 - pasta
 - mozzarella
Directions:
 1. You can start with boiling water for pasta
 2. In pan start warm up on small fire Virgin olive oil, chopped tomatoes and black olives, little salt and basil
 3. When water for pasta start boiling add spoon of salt and pasta to cook
 4. Things in pan should boil for about 2-3 mins
 5. When pasta is ready put it to the pan and mix it all also with chopped mozzarella
 6. You can start to serve
